Network studies are observational research studies conducted across institutions.  Network studies form the foundation of reproducible science with published protocols, computable cohort definitions, analysis, and results.

BETA VERSION 

The network dashboard is based upon automatic parsing of OHDSI/network-studies and may show incomplete information. It does not include all studies conducted or completed in the OHDSI community. If you would like to understand the parsing algorithm used for this data harvesting and assist in gathering complete information, please reach out to nagy@ohdsi.org
